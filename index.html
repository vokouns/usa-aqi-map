This is an AQI (Air Quality Index) to show the air quality in the US, from good

to hazardous. Air quality measures the concentration of pollutants in the air

at a specific time and place. Poor air quality is hazy and can harm human health

and the environment. It's important to note that air quality can change rapidly,

even from hour to hour. This helps to show sensitive groups when to be careful

in times of bad air quality, keeping them alert and responsive.
